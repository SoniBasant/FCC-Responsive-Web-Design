CDS General Bipin Rawat

People's General

General Bipin Rawat was the first Chief of Defence Staff (CDS) and Permanent Chairman of Chiefs of Staff Committee (COSC). As a first CDS of tri-services, he restructured the organization and made a clear path for the combine functioning of all three forces. Making next generation war strategy, modernizing Indian armed forces, promoting made-in-India products for self-reliance are one of the examplary services for our country, India, and shows his future oriented and progressive thinking.

But the reasons because of which he became most loved general are to enhance the morale of the armed forces, vocal for the nationalism and boost respect of armed forces in the eyes of the people. Whether it was Myanmar strikes, Doklam stand off, supported and awarded an army major who, to save his man from lynching, use an insurgent as a shield, highlighted two front war; he was always applauded by the nationalists.

A couple of statements of "People's General" Bipin Rawat

I have to maintain the morale of my troops who are operating there.
